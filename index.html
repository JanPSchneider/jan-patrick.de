About me

Born in 1997 I'm currently studying Internet of Things – Design of Networked Systems at the University of Design in Schwäbisch Gmünd.

After graduating with my A-Level degree in 2015 at the Hans-Baldung-Gymnasium in Schwäbisch Gmünd, I made a voluntary social year in the Field of Culture at the Bureau of Culture Schwäbisch Gmünd.

After studying one semester at the Aalen university I started studying in Schwäbisch Gmünd.

navilight

an intelligent curve light system

Our task was to build an navigation system without the use of symbols and language.

We used the Designsprint method to get a prototyp in five days for an specific usecase and to test it with usertests.
For bikers, who are riding their bike at night. The navigation should work like an foresighted bend lighting. For example you can see here some visualizations:
