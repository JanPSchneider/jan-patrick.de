About me

Born in 1997 I am currently studying Internet of Things – Design of Networked Systems at the University of Design in Schwäbisch Gmünd.

After graduating with my A-Level degree in 2015 at the Hans-Baldung-Gymnasium in Schwäbisch Gmünd, I made a voluntary social year in the Field of Culture at the Bureau of Culture Schwäbisch Gmünd.

After studying one semester at the Aalen university I started studying in Schwäbisch Gmünd.
