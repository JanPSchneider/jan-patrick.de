sense-chess

chess redesign

A chess system for
improved gameplay of beginners and
for intelligent analysis and training.

The problem is that chess only involves a certain amount of challenge when players with different skill level play against each other. Either is is too easy or too hard.
Because in conventional chess games of an advanced against a beginner, the advanced feels under-challenged. And if he shows his skills, this leads to frustration of the beginner.

Also you don't have the possibility to gain information and insights about your gameplay and tactics - the whole training aspect.

Looking like the chessboard at home

Very important is the transferability of the game and it's rules from one board to another. That is why the board is looking pretty normal and the technology is hidden and invisible.

You can focus on learning and playing chess without any distraction.

The transferability of the chessmen is very important. So we designed them with common characteristics still modern and clean.

You can have a look at the documentation, visit the projects website or view the code on GitHub.
