digital twin

exhibition design

The digital twin refers to a digital replica of physical assets (physical twin), processes, people, places, systems and devices that can be used for various purposes. It is one of the first possible IoT use case in industry.

That is why it is that important for the Internet of Things (IoT) and why we choose to make an exhibit about.

This project is still under construction.
