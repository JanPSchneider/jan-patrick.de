Our task was to build an navigation system without the use of symbols and language.

We used the Designsprint method to get a prototyp in five days for an specific usecase and to test it with usertests. For bikers, who are riding their bike at night. The navigation should work like an foresighted bend lighting. For example you can see here some visualizations:
